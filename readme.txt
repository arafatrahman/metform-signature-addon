# MetForm Signature Addon

The **MetForm Signature Addon** is a powerful extension for the MetForm plugin that lets you effortlessly add signature fields to your forms. It’s the perfect solution for collecting digital signatures for contracts, agreements, consent forms, and more.

---

## Features

- **Seamless Integration**: Easily integrates with MetForm to add signature fields.
- **Responsive Design**: Works flawlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
- **Customizable Options**: Adjust signature field styles and behavior to suit your needs.
- **Secure and Encrypted**: Ensures the privacy and security of collected signatures.
- **Versatile Use Cases**: Suitable for contracts, waivers, feedback forms, and other digital signature requirements.

---

## Installation

1. **Download the Plugin**:
   - Download the `.zip` file of the MetForm Signature Addon.
2. **Upload to WordPress**:
   - Go to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to `Plugins > Add New`, and click `Upload Plugin`.
   - Select the `.zip` file and click `Install Now`.
3. **Activate the Plugin**:
   - Once installed, click `Activate Plugin`.
4. **Ensure MetForm is Installed**:
   - This addon requires the MetForm plugin to work. Make sure it’s installed and activated.

---

## Usage

1. **Add Signature Field**:
   - Open the MetForm editor and add a new form or edit an existing one.
   - Drag and drop the "Signature" field into your form.
2. **Customize Field Settings**:
   - Adjust label, placeholder text, and other field settings as needed.
3. **Publish the Form**:
   - Save your form and embed it on your website using the provided shortcode.
4. **View Collected Signatures**:
   - Access submitted forms from the MetForm entries section, including the digital signature.

---

## Requirements

- **WordPress Version**: 5.6 or higher.
- **MetForm Plugin**: Version X.X or higher.
- **PHP Version**: 7.4 or higher.
